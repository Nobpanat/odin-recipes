Creamy Shrimp Tom Yum Soup

Creamy Shrimp Tom Yum Soup is food from Thailand

Ingredient

  • Shrimp, size depends on your preference, 6 tbsp
  • Galangal, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves 1 bunch
  • 10 hot peppers
  • 7 chili peppers
  • 1 spoon chili paste
  • 7 spoons of fresh milk
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 spoonful of sugar
  • 2 spoons of lemon
  • shallots 6 small heads
  • parsley, as you like
  • mushroom as you like

Steps

  1. Peel the shrimp and cut the back to remove the black line
  2. Take the galangal, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves and wash them clean. Then slice and prepareShallots are peeled and washed thoroughly, then smashed slightly.Clean the peppers. Then bring Jinda chili to crush it.Parsley, cleaned and chopped.Slice the mushrooms and wash them clean.
  3. 400 ml of water, bring to a boil, then add galangal, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, shallots, boil for a while and then add mushrooms. When it boils, follow with shrimp.
  4. The prawns start to turn red, let's start seasoning. Add 1 spoon of roasted chili, 7 spoons of fresh milk, followed by adding chili, depending on how spicy you like it, less spicy. Followed by 2 spoons of fish sauce, 1 spoon of sugar, 2 spoons of lime juice, taste according to the taste you like. Sprinkle with paprika and parsley
